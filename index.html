About Me

Born and raised in the suburb of Sendai, Japan. Since when I was a little, I've always been fascinated with the outside of my world. The way other people live and dress, the language they speak, and the authentic food they have at home. I am definitely the person who enjoys and appreciates the differences, "A point or way in which people or things are dissimilar"
because that's how we really get to know who we are, by learning the differences.
I majored in Economics at Tohoku University and have worked in sales operations
at an apparel company for six years (in Singapore, Japan, and the United States).
The more differences and gaps I saw, the more problems and challenges arose.
So I've studied software development at App Academy and I build apps to solve problems.
My learning comes from books, people, and travels. My coding is how I put these together.
